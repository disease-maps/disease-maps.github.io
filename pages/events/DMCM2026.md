The 10th Disease Maps Community Meeting is organised by the [Department of Systems Biology and Bioinformatics](https://www.sbi.uni-rostock.de/) at the University of Rostock, Germany.

Subscribe to our [community mailing list](https://disease-maps.github.io/contact/) to stay tuned with the meeting news.

## Key dates

Abstract submission starts: May 2026  
Abstract submission deadline: August 31, 2026  
Registration deadline: August 31, 2026  

## Registration

Registration is now open via the online form [here](https://forms.cloud.microsoft/r/qp27B49h8u).

Participation in the meeting is **free of charge**, but registration is required.  
Please complete your registration by **August 31, 2026**.

## Abstract submission

Will be available soon.

## Venue

The conference takes place in the historic main building of the University of Rostock, right in the heart of the city center. The building itself is more than 150 years old, and the university, founded in 1419, is one of the oldest in Northern Europe.

## Travel

Rostock can be reached by a direct train connection from Hamburg or Berlin, both of which have major international airports.
