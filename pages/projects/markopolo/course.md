# MARKOPOLO DM & SBGN Course at LIH
## Disease Maps & Systems Biology Graphical Notation

MARKOPOLO DM & SBGN Course: this is an intensive two-week course in a workshop format focused on learning the Disease Maps (DM) concept and the Systems Biology Graphical Notation (SBGN) offered at the Luxembourg Institute of Health (LIH) within the EU-funded MARKOPOLO project.
