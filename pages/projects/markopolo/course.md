# Disease Maps & Systems Biology Graphical Notation Course

MARKOPOLO DM & SBGN Course at LIH: this is an instensive two-week course in a workshop format focused on learning the Disease Maps concept and the Systems Biology Graphical Notation offered at the Luxembourg Institute of Health within the EU-funded MARKOPOLO project.
