# Overlays
## A guide for adding overlays to the map for data visualisation

This page is a step-by-step guide about how to upload a custom data overlay to the ExposomeMap-PM resource for omics data visualation. A general guide to the MINERVA Platform is available at [https://minerva.pages.uni.lu](https://minerva.pages.uni.lu/).

### Step 1

Create an account. For that, you can contact the MINERVA team at [minerva@uni.lu](mailto:minerva@uni.lu). Optionally, you can use your ORCID ID to create an account. Normally your new account will be activated within 24 hours.

For login or sign up, click the icon in the top-left corner.

<img src="/pages/projects/markopolo/images/overlays02.png" width="600" />

Next, sing in via ORCID and submit.

<img src="/pages/projects/markopolo/images/overlays03.png" width="600" />

### Step 2

Prepare the data file. It should be in txt format and consist of two columns "name" and "value", where "name" column inludes protein or gene names as in HUGO Gene Nomenclature Committee and "value" column countains values from -1 to 1.
