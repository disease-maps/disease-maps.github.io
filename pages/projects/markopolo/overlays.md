# Overlays
## A guide for adding overlays to the map for data visualisation

This page is a step-by-step guide about how to upload a custom data overlay to the ExposomeMap-PM resource for omics data visualation. A general guide to the MINERVA Platform is available at [https://minerva.pages.uni.lu](https://minerva.pages.uni.lu/).

### Step 1. Create an account

To create an account, please contact the MINERVA team at [minerva@uni.lu](mailto:minerva@uni.lu). Optionally, you can use your ORCID ID to create an account. Normally your new account will be activated within 24 hours.

For login or sign up, click the icon in the top-left corner.

<img src="/pages/projects/markopolo/images/overlays02.png" width="600" />

Next, sing in via ORCID and submit.

<img src="/pages/projects/markopolo/images/overlays03.png" width="600" />

### Step 2 Prepare data file

The data file should be in txt format and consist of two columns "name" and "value", where "name" column inludes protein or gene names as in HUGO Gene Nomenclature Committee and "value" column countains values from -1 to 1. 

<img src="/pages/projects/markopolo/images/overlays01.png" width="400" />

### Step 3 Upload the data file to overlays

Click the blue "Overlays" button on the top-middle of the window. Click "Add overlay".

<img src="/pages/projects/markopolo/images/overlays04.png" width="600" />

In the next tab, chose your file and create file name.

<img src="/pages/projects/markopolo/images/overlays05.png" width="600" />

Successfuly uploaded file will be visiable as a new (not public) overlay. 

<img src="/pages/projects/markopolo/images/overlays06.png" width="600" />

From this pont you can see your date on the map by chosing the correponding overlay or several overlays.

Optionally, you can go to "Admin panel" and make your ovelay public.

<img src="/pages/projects/markopolo/images/overlays07.png" width="600" />

More information on data upload is availabel at [MINERVA Pages](https://minerva.pages.uni.lu/manuals/v19.0/examples/03-data-overlay-upload).
